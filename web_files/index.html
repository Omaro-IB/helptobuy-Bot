Eastlight is proud to offer 3 x two bedroom houses in the new Rivertree Properties developement at Tilekiln Green in Great Hallingbury. The properties are due to become available for occupation in September 2022 so we are now accepting off-plan reservations. *please note that we are not able to offer viewings until we have taken handover of the proeprties, currently expected to take place at the end of August*

Situated on the outskirts of Bishop's Stortford, the village is ideally situated to combine modern living and comfort. With Stansted airport a short 10 minute drive away and both M11 and M25 in close proximity, the village also enjoys a wide range of woodland and nature walks and trails.
